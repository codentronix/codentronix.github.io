In this tutorial I will show you how to write to a Character LCD using an arduino microcontroller. This will be the first part of a series of tutorials on driving Character LCDs. In this first part I will keep the things simple and easy. I will just show you how to wire the LCD to the Arduino, and write some text to it.

[caption id="" align="alignnone" width="400" caption="Example of a Character LCD"][/caption]

What you will need:
1x 1 Arduino Board.
1x 1 HD44780 Character LCD.
Some wires.
1x 1.5K Resistor
