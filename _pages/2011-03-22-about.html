codeNtronix is a site dedicated to all who have interest in programming and electronics. The site will cover topics such as mobile programming, web programming, low-level programming, microcontroller programming, electronics circuit design among others. Several programming languages ​​and platforms will be covered.
This site was created on April 7th, 2011 by Leonel Machava, a geek from Mozambique.

About Leonel

Hi, I am Leonel Machava. I have a strong passion for computing and electronics, and I am self-taught in these two subjects. I started programming when I was 13 years old. I used QuickBasic and soon migrated to Visual Basic 6. Now 11 years later, I master Assembly, Java, C, C++, Python, PHP, VB.NET, C#, Pascal, Prolog,Camlight, among others.

My areas of interest in computing and electronics are:

  • Computer Graphics (2D and 3D)
  • Game Programming
  • Mobile Applications
  • Web Applications
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Operating System Development
  • Programming Language ​​Design and Development of Interpreters and Compilers
  • Design and Development of Distributed Systems
  • Security of Information Systems
  • Electronics Circuit Design
  • Design and Development of Embedded Systems.

The articles that I write on this site are related to the categories listed above.
